Pokemon case for games

Keep your Pokemon series Gameboy games safe and organized with this exclusive case designed for true Pokemon trainers! This case is made of durable materials and provides solid protection for your game cartridges, ensuring they are safe from scratches, dust, and accidental damage.

Multiple cartridge capacity: With the ability to store up to 12 Game Boy game cartridges, this case allows you to carry a wide selection of your favorite Pokemon games with you.

Tetris Game Boy Display Stand

Show your love for the classic Game Boy Tetris game with this elegant display stand. This stand is specifically designed to highlight and showcase your Game Boy console while you play or when you're not using it.

Perfect for display: When you're not playing, this display stand is ideal for showcasing your Game Boy, adding a retro and nostalgic touch to your gaming area or collection shelf.

Handyboy

The HandyBoy is an essential accessory for Game Boy enthusiasts looking to take their gaming experience to the next level. This multifunctional device is designed to provide comfort, functionality, and versatility to Game Boy users.

Screen amplifier and built-in speaker: The HandyBoy features an amplified screen that enhances the visibility of your Game Boy, providing a more immersive gaming experience. Additionally, it includes built-in speakers for improved audio quality, taking the sound of your games to another level.

Gameboy Camera

The Game Boy Camera is an item that provides a unique photography experience for Game Boy enthusiasts and collectors. Designed as a fun and creative accessory for the console, this compact camera offers a distinctive way of capturing moments.

Photo printing compatibility: You can print your photos using the Game Boy Printer (sold separately), adding an extra touch of authenticity to your digital memories by turning them into tangible photographs.
